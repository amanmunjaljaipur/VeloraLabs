# Content framework (App Content Agent)

## Voice

- Friendly shopkeeper + clear coach  
- Class-8 English; short paragraphs  
- Specific to **this** city and offer  
- Premium = concrete care, not luxury jargon  

## Page jobs

| Page | Visitor job | Must include |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Home | Want to browse / order | Hero headline, sub, CTA, trust, featured products |
| Products | Choose item | Clear names, prices, photo, order path |
| About | Trust the owner | Story, city, what you stand for |
| FAQ | Unblock order | Pay, deliver, hours, how to order |
| Contact | Reach human | Phone / WhatsApp / address / hours |

## Section formulas

**Hero headline**  
`{Outcome or welcome} · {Brand or city cue}`  
Example: “Fresh pickles from our Jaipur kitchen”

**Hero sub**  
Who it’s for + how to order in one sentence.

**About**  
2–3 short HTML paragraphs: who we are → what we make → how customers buy.

**FAQ**  
Question as the customer would ask; answer with method (WhatsApp, UPI, pickup).

## Premiumization checklist

1. Replace vague adjectives with product/city facts  
2. One benefit per sentence  
3. Trust line from real methods only  
4. CTA verb matches action (“See products”, “Message on WhatsApp”)  
5. Footer note human: `© year brand · city`  

## Multi-colour theme note for copy

Do not write “our blue site.” Write mood words that fit a multi-colour brand (warm, bold, calm garden, coastal). UI applies `primary` / `secondary` / `accent` / gradients separately.
